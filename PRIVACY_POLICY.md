# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to MiiMii ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, website, WhatsApp services, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

- **Identity Information**: Name, date of birth, gender, government-issued identification numbers (BVN)
- **Contact Information**: Phone number, email address, physical address
- **Financial Information**: Bank account details, transaction history, wallet balance, virtual account information
- **Authentication Information**: PIN, password (hashed), biometric data (if applicable)
- **Profile Information**: Profile photo, preferences, settings
- **Communication Data**: Messages sent through our chat services, support tickets

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Usage Information**: How you interact with our Services, features used, time spent, pages visited
- **Location Information**: General location data (with your permission)
- **Technical Information**: IP address, browser type, access times, referring URLs
- **Transaction Information**: Transaction amounts, dates, types, beneficiaries, status

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

- **Financial Institutions**: Bank account verification, transaction confirmations
- **Identity Verification Services**: BVN verification, KYC providers
- **Payment Processors**: Transaction processing information
- **Service Providers**: Data plans, airtime, utility bill providers

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
- **Authentication**: Multi-factor authentication and PIN protection for sensitive operations
- **Secure Storage**: Personal information stored in secure databases with access restrictions
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Monitoring**: Continuous monitoring for security threats and breaches

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
