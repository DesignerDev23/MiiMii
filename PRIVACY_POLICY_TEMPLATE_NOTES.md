# Privacy Policy Creation Notes

## Overview
This document provides guidance on creating and customizing the privacy policy for MiiMii. The main privacy policy template is in `PRIVACY_POLICY.md`.

## Key Sections to Customize

### 1. Contact Information (Section 13)
**Action Required**: Update with actual contact details
- Replace `privacy@miimii.ai` with your actual privacy email
- Replace phone number placeholder with real number
- Add your business address
- Update support portal URL if different

### 2. Business Address (Section 13)
**Action Required**: Add your registered business address
```markdown
**Address**: [Your Business Address]
```

### 3. Phone Number (Section 13)
**Action Required**: Add customer support phone number
```markdown
**Phone**: +234 XXX XXX XXXX
```

### 4. Regulatory Compliance (Section 14)
**Action Required**: Verify applicable regulations
- Confirm NDPR compliance requirements
- Add any other relevant regulations (CBN guidelines, etc.)
- Update if operating in other jurisdictions

### 5. Data Retention Period (Section 7)
**Action Required**: Verify retention periods
- Current: 7 years for financial records (standard)
- Confirm with legal/compliance team
- May vary by jurisdiction

### 6. Age Restriction (Section 8)
**Action Required**: Confirm minimum age
- Current: 18 years
- May need to adjust based on local laws
- Consider adding age verification process

## Legal Review Checklist

Before publishing, ensure:

- [ ] Reviewed by legal counsel
- [ ] Compliance with NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation)
- [ ] Compliance with CBN guidelines (if applicable)
- [ ] Compliance with GDPR (if serving EU users)
- [ ] All contact information is accurate
- [ ] Data retention periods are correct
- [ ] Third-party service disclosures are complete
- [ ] Security measures accurately described
- [ ] User rights section is comprehensive

## Implementation Steps

### Step 1: Customize Content
1. Update all placeholder information
2. Add specific details about your services
3. Include all third-party services you use
4. Specify exact data retention periods

### Step 2: Legal Review
1. Have legal counsel review the document
2. Ensure compliance with all applicable laws
3. Verify accuracy of all statements
4. Confirm all required disclosures are included

### Step 3: Technical Integration
1. Add privacy policy link to:
   - Mobile app (Settings/About section)
   - Website footer
   - Registration/signup flow
   - Terms of Service page
2. Implement consent mechanism (if required)
3. Add version tracking

### Step 4: User Communication
1. Notify existing users of policy updates
2. Require acceptance for new users
3. Provide easy access to policy
4. Make policy easily readable and accessible

## Third-Party Services to Disclose

Update Section 11 with actual third-party services:

- **Payment Processors**: Rubies, BellBank, etc.
- **Cloud Services**: Digital Ocean, AWS, etc.
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics, etc.
- **Communication**: WhatsApp Business API, SMS providers
- **Identity Verification**: BVN verification services
- **Data Plans/Airtime**: Service providers
- **Support Tools**: Customer support platforms

## Data Collection Points

Ensure all data collection points are disclosed:

1. **Registration/Signup**
   - Email, phone, name, password

2. **KYC Process**
   - BVN, date of birth, address, identification documents

3. **Transaction Processing**
   - Transaction details, beneficiary information

4. **App Usage**
   - Device information, usage patterns, location (if enabled)

5. **Support/Chat**
   - Messages, support tickets, communication history

6. **WhatsApp Integration**
   - WhatsApp number, messages, profile information

## Security Measures to Highlight

Update Section 5 with actual security measures:

- SSL/TLS encryption
- PIN protection
- Multi-factor authentication
- Secure database storage
- Regular security audits
- Access controls
- Monitoring and alerting systems

## User Rights Implementation

Ensure you can actually fulfill the rights listed in Section 6:

- [ ] Data export functionality
- [ ] Account deletion process
- [ ] Data correction mechanisms
- [ ] Opt-out mechanisms
- [ ] Access request handling

## Version Control

- Maintain version history
- Track changes and dates
- Archive previous versions
- Notify users of material changes

## Accessibility

- Make policy available in multiple formats (web, PDF, app)
- Use clear, simple language
- Provide translations if serving multiple regions
- Ensure mobile-friendly format

## Regular Updates

Review and update the privacy policy:
- Annually (minimum)
- When adding new services
- When changing data practices
- When required by law
- When third-party services change

## Additional Considerations
